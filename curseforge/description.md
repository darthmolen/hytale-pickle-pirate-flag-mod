# Pickle Pirate Flag

Plant your flag and claim your territory! A decorative flagpole featuring a pickle pirate skull design with animated wave motion.

## Features

- **Plantable Flagpole** - Place anywhere in the world
- **Wave Animation** - Flag waves naturally in the wind
- **Map Marker** - Shows on the world map to mark your locations
- **Rename via Map** - Right-click the marker on the map to rename your flag
- **Custom Textures** - Unique pickle pirate skull and crossbones design

## Installation

### Server Installation

1. Download both the plugin JAR and asset pack
2. Place the JAR in your server's `mods/` folder
3. Extract the asset pack to `mods/PicklePirateFlag/`
4. Restart the server

### Single Player

1. Subscribe via CurseForge app (auto-installs)

## Crafting

Craft at a **Workbench (Tier 1)**:
- 20x Wood Sticks
- 10x Linen

## Usage

1. Craft the Pickle Pirate Flag at a Workbench
2. Place it in the world like any block
3. The flag will appear on your world map
4. Right-click the map marker to rename your flag

## Requirements

- Hytale Early Access or later
- Server with mod support enabled
